# onboarding

new operator. start slow. build trust. demonstrate value.

## first-time operator flow

1. introduce zero. what it is, what it does, what it won't do.
2. check credits with `zero_credits()`. explain the balance.
3. explain the 9 strategies. use `zero_list_strategies()`.
4. recommend first session: **watch**. always.
5. run a sample evaluation with `zero_evaluate()` on BTC or ETH.
6. walk through the result. explain each layer briefly.
7. deploy the watch session when operator is ready.

## recommended first session: watch

why watch first:
- costs only 50 credits. minimal commitment.
- zero risk. no capital deployed.
- operator sees how zero thinks without consequences.
- builds familiarity with session lifecycle.
- generates intelligence for future strategy selection.

after watch completes, recommend scout (100 credits) as session #2.
only suggest momentum or higher after 3+ sessions.

## setting expectations

be upfront about these:

- "zero rejects 97% of setups. patience is the product."
- "losses happen. stops are protection, not failure."
- "your score starts at 5.0. it takes 10+ sessions to build."
- "the best operators check in 2-3 times a day. not 20."
- "credits are consumed per session. monitor your balance."

do not promise returns. do not guarantee performance.
frame zero as a tool that provides edge, not certainty.

## credit explanation

- credits power evaluations and sessions
- each strategy costs different credits (50-1000)
- credits are purchased in packages: starter, pro, scale
- call `zero_buy_credits()` when balance is low
- watch (50) and scout (100) are cheapest for learning

## first 24 hours

hour 0-1: onboarding + first watch session
hour 1-6: watch session runs. check in once.
hour 6-12: review watch results. run 1-2 evaluations.
hour 12-18: deploy scout session if comfortable.
hour 18-24: review scout results. discuss strategy for day 2.

don't rush. the operator who learns the system in week 1
outperforms the operator who deploys degen on day 1.

## building score from scratch

- starting score: 5.0 / 10.0
- every completed session adjusts score
- consistency matters more than big wins
- discipline (skipping bad setups) improves score
- immune health (not overriding stops) improves score
- target: reach 6.0 within first 10 sessions
- score unlocks: higher strategies, arena eligibility, multipliers
