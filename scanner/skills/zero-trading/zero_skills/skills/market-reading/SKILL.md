# market reading

data first. opinion never. let the layers speak.

## 7-layer evaluation

every coin passes through 7 intelligence layers before a verdict:

1. **regime detection** — what market state are we in
2. **trend analysis** — multi-timeframe directional bias
3. **momentum scoring** — strength and acceleration of moves
4. **volatility profiling** — expected range and spike probability
5. **funding/sentiment** — crowd positioning and cost of carry
6. **correlation mapping** — how this coin moves relative to BTC, ETH, sector
7. **consensus engine** — weighted agreement across all layers

call `zero_evaluate(coin)` to get all 7 layers in one response.

## reading the heat map

conviction bars show strength. direction arrows show bias.

- conviction 0.0-0.3 → no edge. skip.
- conviction 0.3-0.6 → possible setup. needs confirmation.
- conviction 0.6-0.8 → solid setup. worth deploying.
- conviction 0.8-1.0 → high conviction. rare. act decisively.

arrows: ↑ long bias, ↓ short bias, → neutral/unclear.

## regimes

regimes define what strategies work. never fight the regime.

- **trending** — directional moves. momentum and apex thrive.
- **chaotic** — random, noisy. only watch or scout. no edge.
- **stable** — low vol, range-bound. funding and fade work.
- **mean-reverting** — oscillating. fade is king. momentum fails.

## consensus interpretation

consensus is the weighted agreement of all 7 layers.

- strong_bullish (> 0.7) → layers agree. long bias.
- bullish (0.4-0.7) → mild agreement. needs confirmation.
- neutral (-0.3 to 0.3) → no agreement. stay out.
- bearish (-0.7 to -0.4) → mild bearish. defense or fade.
- strong_bearish (< -0.7) → layers agree. short bias or exit.

## fear & greed context

market-wide sentiment affects all coins.

- extreme fear → contrarian opportunities. but wait for confirmation.
- fear → defense and funding strategies work well.
- neutral → follow the layers. no sentiment edge.
- greed → momentum works but watch for exhaustion.
- extreme greed → fade setups appear. be cautious with longs.

## rules

- never trade against strong consensus without a clear thesis
- chaotic regime = sit on hands. patience pays
- always check correlation to BTC before isolated coin trades
- conviction < 0.3 means zero has no edge. respect that
